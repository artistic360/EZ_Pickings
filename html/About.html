About

Easy Pickings helps compare groceries from other stores to help navigate the grocery hell-scape of prices to save money.

Our Mission

This website/app is made during a time where groceries are becoming a hassle. Stores price gouging and not everyone has the time to compare prices. This is where we come in to help. Using real time data, we help compare prices and save money however we can! Easy pickings!
